# OP2 Plan Template (Operating Plan — Second Half)

> Amazon Operating Plan document for H2. Covers goals, initiatives, investments, and headcount for the second half of the year.
> Body sections must be narrative prose. Tables and bullets are permitted in the Investments, Headcount, and Appendix sections.
> OP2 documents are reviewed by senior leadership. Be specific, honest about gaps, and clear about what you are asking for.

---

## Executive Summary

*Purpose: Give a senior leader everything they need in 3-4 sentences. What did H1 look like, what are the H2 priorities, and what are you asking for?*
*Target: ~0.25 page*

[Write a tight 3-4 sentence summary. H1 performance (honest), top 2-3 H2 priorities, and the headline investment or headcount ask.]

---

## H1 Retrospective

*Purpose: Honest review of what happened in H1 against the H1 plan.*
*Target: ~0.75 page*
*Do not just list wins. Acknowledge what missed, why, and what was learned.*

[Write 3-4 paragraphs covering: what was committed to in H1, what was achieved, what was missed and why, and the key lesson that informs H2 planning.]

---

## H2 Goals

*Purpose: The measurable outcomes you are committing to in H2.*
*Target: ~0.5 page*
*Goals must be specific and measurable with a clear metric and target. "Improve customer satisfaction" is not a goal. "Increase NPS from 42 to 55 by December 31" is.*

[Write prose describing each goal. For each, state: what the metric is, the current baseline, the H2 target, and why this goal was chosen.]
