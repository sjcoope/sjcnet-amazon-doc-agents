# 2-Pager Template

> A concise proposal or recommendation document. Target: 2 pages of dense prose.
> All body sections must be narrative prose — no bullets.
> Use this for feature pitches, project proposals, or decision documents that do not need the full depth of a 6-pager.

---

# [Document Title]

**Date:** [Date]
**Author:** [Name]
**Status:** Draft / Review / Final
**Distribution:** [Team or Org]

---

## Problem or Opportunity

*Purpose: Define the problem you are solving or the opportunity you are pursuing. Why does this matter, and why now?*
*Target: ~0.5 page (2 paragraphs)*
*Be specific — quantify the problem. Who is affected, to what degree, and what is the cost of inaction?*

[Write 2 paragraphs. First paragraph: what the problem or opportunity is, with specific data. Second paragraph: why this matters now and what happens if nothing is done.]

---

## Proposed Solution

*Purpose: Describe what you are proposing to do.*
*Target: ~0.75 page (3 paragraphs)*
*Explain the approach, not just the outcome. Be clear about what is in scope and what is not.*

[Write 3 paragraphs describing the solution. Cover: what it is, how it works at a high level, and what makes this the right approach over alternatives.]

---

## Expected Impact

*Purpose: What will change as a result of this? Make it measurable.*
*Target: ~0.5 page (2 paragraphs)*
*Connect impact back to the problem defined in the first section.*

[Write 2 paragraphs. First: customer or business impact with specific metrics. Second: any risks or trade-offs the reader should know about.]

---

## Ask and Next Steps

*Purpose: What do you need from the reader, and what happens next?*
*Target: ~0.25 page (1 paragraph)*
*Be explicit about the ask: approval, headcount, budget, a decision, feedback.*

[One clear paragraph stating exactly what you are asking for and what the immediate next steps are if approved.]
